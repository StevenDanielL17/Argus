Developer Roles

•
Product Manager (PM): Bridges business goals with technical execution.

•
Frontend Developer: Builds the user interface (UI) and user experience (UX).

•
Backend Developer: Manages server-side logic, databases, and APIs.

•
DevOps Engineer: Automates deployment, manages infrastructure and CI/CD.

•
QA (Quality Assurance) Engineer: Focuses on testing and ensuring product quality.

•
UI/UX Designer: Creates visual designs and maps out user journeys.
